Lardo

Lardo has a sweet, subtle pork flavor and a texture not unlike butter. It's best eaten in very thin slices so the fat can melt over the tongue. Aside from a rich umami flavor, lardo has nuances of the herbs used to cure it.

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • onions

Steps

  1. Cook lardo with potatoes (or any starchy vegetable) and let the fat melt in just before serving
  2. Lardo has a high smoke point, so frying with it works is another option.
  3. Lardo can even cubed and cut into pastry dough.
